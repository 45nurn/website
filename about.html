Our History

A journey of growth and service to our community

1985

Foundation

MediCare Hospital was founded with 50 beds and a vision to provide quality healthcare to the growing community.

1995

First Expansion

Expanded to 150 beds and added specialized departments including cardiology and pediatrics.

2005

Advanced Technology

Implemented state-of-the-art medical technology including MRI and robotic surgery systems.

2012

New Cancer Center

Opened the region's most advanced cancer treatment center with comprehensive care services.

2020

Pandemic Response

Led the community response to COVID-19, establishing dedicated treatment facilities and vaccination centers.

2023

Modernization Complete

Completed a $150 million modernization project, featuring a new emergency department and patient tower.
