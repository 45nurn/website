Advanced Diagnostic Technology

State-of-the-art equipment for precise eye examinations and treatment

OCT Imaging

Optical Coherence Tomography provides detailed cross-sectional images of the retina for early detection of diseases like macular degeneration and glaucoma.

Retinal Photography

Ultra-widefield imaging captures 200° of the retina in a single image, allowing comprehensive evaluation and documentation of retinal health.

Corneal Topography

Advanced mapping of the corneal surface for precise planning of refractive surgery and diagnosis of corneal diseases.
