Advanced Diagnostic Services

State-of-the-art neurological diagnostics for accurate assessment

MRI & CT Scans

High-resolution imaging for detailed brain and spinal cord visualization with 3T MRI technology.

EEG & EMG

Electroencephalogram and electromyography for assessing brain activity and nerve function.

Neurogenetic Testing

Advanced genetic testing for hereditary neurological conditions and personalized treatment.

Lumbar Puncture

Cerebrospinal fluid analysis for diagnosing infections, inflammation, and neurological disorders.
